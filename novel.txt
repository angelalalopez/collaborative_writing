He didn't know why they were there... but they were.
They were both freightening and exciting!
The air reeked of ozone and cheap boxed wine.
And then he remembered the invitation from long ago; they'd finally arrived. 
He had finally finished. He had finally arrived after one, long, arduous minute.
And then the silence became eternal. 
The eternal silence was broken by a deafening alarm clock.
Gawking at it, Billy Bob Thornton realized he was no longer wearing his favourite leather jacket.
He looked around his treehouse where he fell asleep and found himself lying in his onesie instead. 
Startled by the Onesie, he decided to look for his Shotgun. Along with his partner, Arnold Schwarzenneger.
Then shots where fired ...
